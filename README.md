# The Sword of Neramar

A text-based, choice-driven adventure game built with Node.js.

## Overview

In _The Sword of Neramar_, you play as a brave adventurer on a quest to claim the legendary Sword of Neramar. Your decisions shape the story, from exploring forests and caves to fighting goblins, robbers, and a dragon.

## Features

- Interactive branching story with multiple paths
- Weapon choice at the start: sword, spear, bow, or mace
- Combat encounters with goblins, robbers, and a dragon
- Shop and village mechanics with gold, health, and armor
- Multiple endings based on your choices
- Help and quit commands available at any time

## Requirements

- Node.js installed (version 12 or later recommended)

## Running the Game

1. Open a terminal in the project folder.
2. Run:

```bash
node game.js
```

## How to Play

- Follow the prompts on-screen.
- Answer choices by typing one of the provided options and pressing Enter.
- Common commands available during gameplay:
  - `help` - display instructions and return to your previous scene
  - `quit` - attempt to exit the game

## Gameplay Notes

- You start with full health and no armor.
- You can regain health by choosing `camp` or `rest` when available.
- Gold can be earned from treasure, battles, and quests.
- Use gold to buy health or armor from shops.
- Your ending depends on whether you find the Sword of Neramar and how you navigate the story.

## Story Paths

- Forest: choose between left, right, or camping.
- Cave and underground city exploration.
- Village quests and shop interactions.
- Ruins and dragon encounter.
- Mountain pass and the magical cave.

## Notes

This game runs entirely in the terminal and is designed for a single playthrough session. Enjoy the adventure and choose wisely!
